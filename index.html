RWD- Responsive Wed Design

Responsive Web Design is a webpage that creates a responsive or interactive design that is able to adapt to different screens or devices. It allows webpages to not only look good one diffeent devices but its ensures the universal functionality of the webpage on all devices.

Why is RWD important?

Responsive Web Design is important because in this day and age, indivduals have a multitude of devices they can use to access the internet. RWD allows the users to access webpages on most, if not all of their devices, therefore benefiting not only the user but also the company or individual that runs the webpage.

Process for creatng RWD?

A process to creating responsive web designs normally begin through the design of the webpage in a normal webbrowser for example on the computer, once you have you initial layout you can then start altering the page and content within the page to fit on smaller screen, fro example on tablets or cell phone screens. It is important to test your RWD on multiple devices and screens to ensure the reliability and convince of the lay out of content to ensure it is set out in a user friendly way.
